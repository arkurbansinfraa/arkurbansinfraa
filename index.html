About ARK Urbans Infraa

ARK Urbans Infraa is a trusted real estate development company, recognized by our customers for delivering high-quality residential and commercial spaces.

Since 2003, we have successfully completed 35+ projects across Hyderabad and Bangalore, developing over 500k sq. ft. of standalone properties and creating 500+ satisfied homeowners.

With two decades of experience in real estate development, our focus has always been on quality, trust, and timely delivery. ARK Urbans Infraa was founded to provide modern, turnkey solutions that make home-building simpler, stress-free, and truly customer-friendly.
